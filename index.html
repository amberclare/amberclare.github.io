Why I aspire to become a coder

For most of my professional career I have worked as a secretary. It is a very traditional, female dominated role. It is also a dying field. As with many other industries, much of the work I do as a secretary is becoming automated. From using speech to text recognition instead of transcribing to automated billing my profession is becoming obselete. Having a secretary has always been a luxury for the people
